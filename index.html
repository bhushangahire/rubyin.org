The mission of Ruby India is to build a strong, organized and open community, in Indian subcontinent, of like minded enthusiasts who have either worked on ruby related projects or are interested to use it in future projects. The sole purpose of Ruby India is to organize formal and informal meetups such as meetings, camps, conferences, hackathons etc to build this community successfully.

Meetups

There are a number of regional Ruby-related meetups all over the country. Each group is organised independently by its members, and all are very welcoming to newcomers. The following groups meet regularly:

  • Bangalore
  • Pune
  • Delhi NCR
  • Hyderabad
  • Chennai
  • Kochi

Rails Camp

Ruby India is the organising body of Indian Rails Camp events. Please see the official Rails Camp India site for more details.
